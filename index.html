About Me.A few details

My name is Esther Odiriawodia Metieh. I am a 23years old Christian from Delta State. I am the first and only girl-child of my parents, I have only one sibling and his name is Emmanuel Metieh.

I attended Regina Mundi Girls Secondary School, Iwo, Osun State from 2005 to 2011. I then proceeded to the University of Benin, Edo State where I studied Agricultural Economics from 2012 to 2017.

I am computer literate with profiency in using Microsoft office tools(excel,powerpoint and word). I also have basic knowledge of HTML, CSS and PHP.

I possess articulate communication and information dissemination skills, I am a versatile and creative problem solver with the ability to work in teams and under pressure. I love travelling and meeting new people and I have wonderful friends.
